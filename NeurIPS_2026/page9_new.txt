To test graceful degradation under partial coverage, we evaluate rule-dropout ablations where a
random subset of symbolic rules is removed during training and/or inference. The ablation rate ρ
ranges over {0.25, 0.5, 0.75}. The supplementary material reports how MSEH , return, and RVR
evolve with ρ, and confirms that NS-MAWM maintains a smoother degradation curve than baselines
that do not explicitly separate covered and residual dynamics.
